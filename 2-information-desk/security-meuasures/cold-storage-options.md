# Cold Storage Options

For users seeking additional security, **VAULT Wallet offers cold storage options**. Cold storage involves storing cryptocurrencies offline, away from internet-connected devices, reducing the risk of hacking. Users can transfer their assets to cold storage through the wallet interface. The wallet provides guidance on setting up and managing cold storage solutions. This feature is ideal for long-term holders who prioritize security over accessibility. By offering cold storage options, VAULT Wallet caters to a broader range of security preferences among users.
