# Encryption and Key Management

At VAULT Wallet, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of our users' digital assets with advanced encryption and key management practices. Our dedication to protecting your data is evident in every aspect of our platform:

**State-of-the-Art Encryption:** We employ AES encryption, TLS/SSL protocols, and end-to-end encryption to ensure that your sensitive information remains private both in transit and at rest. This robust encryption framework safeguards your user credentials, transaction details, and communications from unauthorized access.

**Secure Key Management:** Your private keys, vital for accessing and managing your cryptocurrency, are generated and stored locally on your device. This decentralized approach ensures that only you have direct control over your keys, enhancing security by minimizing exposure risks.

**Backup and Recovery:** We provide a 12-word seed phrase during wallet setup, serving as a secure backup to recover your account in case of emergencies. It's crucial to store this seed phrase safely, ensuring continuity of access and peace of mind.

**Continuous Security Audits:** Regular security audits by independent firms and proactive updates keep VAULT Wallet resilient against evolving cyber threats. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of security to protect your assets and personal information.

Experience the confidence of managing your digital assets securely with VAULT Wallet. Our encryption and key management practices are designed to empower you with peace of mind, knowing that your transactions and sensitive data are safeguarded at every step.
