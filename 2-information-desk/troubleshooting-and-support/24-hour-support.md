# 24-Hour Support

VAULT Wallet offers 24-hour messaging support to ensure users can reach customer service at any time. Built on the Telegram platform, this feature enables seamless communication for users seeking assistance or updates. Whether it's resolving technical queries or providing account-related support, users can engage with the support team around the clock. This commitment to continuous availability enhances user convenience and satisfaction, ensuring timely resolutions and support within VAULT Wallet.
