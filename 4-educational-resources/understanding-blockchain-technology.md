# Understanding Blockchain Technology

Blockchain technology is the backbone of cryptocurrencies, and Sharks Wallet provides resources to explain its principles. Users can learn about how blockchains work, their advantages, and their various applications. Detailed articles and videos cover topics such as consensus mechanisms, smart contracts, and decentralized networks. These resources are designed to be informative and engaging, catering to users of all knowledge levels. By offering a deeper understanding of blockchain, Sharks Wallet empowers users to make informed decisions about their crypto activities.
