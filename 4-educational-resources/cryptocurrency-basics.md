# Cryptocurrency Basics

Sharks Wallet offers educational resources to help users understand the basics of cryptocurrency. Topics covered include the history of cryptocurrency, how it works, and its benefits. Simple, jargon-free explanations make the information accessible to beginners. The wallet also provides guides on buying, selling, and storing cryptocurrencies securely. These resources aim to demystify cryptocurrency and encourage more people to explore the space. By educating users, Sharks Wallet fosters a more informed and confident community.\
