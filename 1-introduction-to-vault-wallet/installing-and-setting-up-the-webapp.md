# Installing and Setting up the WebApp

To install VAULT Wallet as a Progressive Web App (PWA), follow these simple steps:

1. **Visit the VAULT Wallet Website**: Open your web browser and navigate to the VAULT Wallet website at stablevault.app.
2. **Access the Install Option**: Once the website loads, you'll see an option to install the app. This can usually be found in the browser's menu or as a prompt at the bottom of the screen.
3. **Install the App**: Click on the "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" button. This action prompts your browser to install VAULT Wallet on your device.
4. **Confirmation**: You may be asked to confirm the installation by clicking "Install" or "Add". This step varies slightly depending on your device and browser.
5. **Shortcut on Home Screen**: After installation, a shortcut icon for VAULT Wallet will appear on your device's home screen or app drawer. You can now access VAULT Wallet directly by tapping on this icon, just like any other app.
6. **Launch and Setup**: Open VAULT Wallet from your home screen. Follow the on-screen instructions to create an account or log in if you already have one. During setup, you may be required to enter your email address and set up security measures like a seed phrase for account recovery.
7. **Start Using VAULT Wallet**: Once set up is complete, you can start managing your crypto assets using VAULT Wallet. Enjoy its features such as viewing balances, sending and receiving cryptocurrencies, and managing transaction historiesâ€”all within a secure and user-friendly interface.

Installing VAULT Wallet as a PWA ensures you have quick access to your wallet directly from your device's home screen, without the need to download it from an app store. It combines the convenience of an app with the flexibility of a web-based platform, accessible on various devices and operating systems.
