# Notification Preferences

VAULT Wallet allows users to tailor their notification preferences to receive timely updates on critical events and activities. From transaction confirmations to security alerts and system updates, users can choose to receive notifications via email, SMS, or push notifications directly to their device.

**Key Features Include:**

1. **Transaction Confirmations:** Receive instant notifications when transactions are executed, ensuring transparency and peace of mind.
2. **Security Alerts:** Stay informed about potential security events or suspicious activities detected within your account.
3. **System Updates:** Get notified about new features, improvements, or maintenance activities that may affect your wallet usage.

**Flexible Delivery Options:**

Users have the flexibility to select their preferred notification delivery method—email, SMS, or push notifications—based on their communication preferences and convenience.

**Easy Adjustment:** Notification settings can be easily managed and adjusted in the profile management section of the wallet interface. This user-friendly approach allows users to maintain control over their notification preferences effortlessly.

**Enhanced User Experience:**

Customized notifications ensure that users stay informed without being overwhelmed by unnecessary alerts. This feature not only enhances user experience by providing relevant updates but also fosters engagement with the wallet's functionalities.

**Security and Convenience:** By keeping users informed about important activities, VAULT Wallet promotes a secure environment while accommodating individual communication preferences.
