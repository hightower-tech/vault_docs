# Stablecoin Management

VAULT **Wallet provides** robust features for managing the three most popular stablecoins: **USDT, USDC, and DAI**, ensuring users can easily handle these widely-used assets. The wallet monitors the stablecoin/USD rate and alerts users to any significant deviations. This helps users stay informed about potential depegging events, protecting their investments. Users can also view their stablecoin balances separately, providing clear insights into their holdings. The wallet's interface simplifies stablecoin transactions, making it easy to send, receive, and convert stablecoins. These features ensure that users can manage their stablecoin assets efficiently and securely.

* **Monitoring stablecoin/USD rates.**
* **Depeg alerts.**
* **Stablecoin Balance View Switcher.**
